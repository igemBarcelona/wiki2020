  • Needs finding survey


    We carried out a survey with the aim of identifying the main challenges faced by a researcher or a laboratory technician in their daily laboratory procedures and, consequently, to propose a synthetic biology approach to face those challenges.

    Output: some of the annotated ideas were: improvement of the laboratory equipment performance, optimization of cloning strategies or other time-consuming strategies and the development of fully automated procedures.

    Adjustment: even though some of the previous ideas were considered, we were balancing other projects with huge potential as well. Nevertheless, the identified daily laboratory challenges can be a great starting point for future iGEM team projects.
